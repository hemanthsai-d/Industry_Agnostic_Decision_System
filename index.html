The Big Picture

What Does This System Do?

Think of it as a brilliant assistant that reads every customer message, instantly understands the problem, finds the best answer from your knowledge base, and — most importantly — knows when it's not sure and should hand off to a human expert.

📩

Reads & Understands

A customer sends a message — by email, chat, or social media. The system instantly reads it, removes any personal information (like credit card numbers), and figures out what the customer needs.

🔎

Finds the Right Answer

It searches through your company's knowledge base — FAQs, policies, runbooks — using advanced AI retrieval to find the most relevant information to solve the problem.

🎯

Makes a Smart Decision

Based on 5 different signals of confidence, it decides: "I can handle this", "I'm not sure enough", or "This needs a human expert". No guessing — every decision is backed by data.

Smart Outcomes

Three Possible Decisions

Unlike simple AI systems that always try to answer, this platform makes nuanced decisions based on how confident it is. This prevents wrong answers from reaching customers.

✅ RECOMMEND High confidence — the AI drafts a response for the agent to review and send
⏸️ ABSTAIN Moderate uncertainty — the AI provides evidence but defers the final answer to a human
🚨 ESCALATE High risk or complexity — immediately routes to a specialist with full context
Why It's Different

5-Signal Confidence Scoring

Instead of a single "how sure am I?" number, the system evaluates 5 different factors to decide whether it should respond, abstain, or escalate. This makes decisions much more reliable.

45%

🎯 Route Confidence

How sure the AI is about what category this problem falls into (e.g., billing, shipping, technical)

25%

📚 Evidence Quality

How relevant and trustworthy the knowledge base articles are that support the answer

20%

⚠️ Escalation Risk

A separate model that predicts whether this case will end up needing human help anyway

−7%

🔍 Out-of-Distribution

Detects unusual messages the AI hasn't seen before — penalizes confidence on unfamiliar topics

−3%

🔀 Contradiction Score

Catches when different pieces of evidence disagree with each other — a sign the AI shouldn't be confident
