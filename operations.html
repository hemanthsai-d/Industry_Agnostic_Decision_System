People

On-Call Structure

Different types of problems need different experts. The system routes alerts to the team that can fix them fastest.

🚒

Platform On-Call

Handles infrastructure issues: servers, databases, networking, deployment.

  • Redis failures
  • Database pool exhaustion
  • Health check failures
  • Disk space alerts
🤖

Model On-Call

Handles AI/ML issues: accuracy, drift, escalation rates, model performance.

  • High abstain rate
  • Low routing accuracy
  • Model drift detected
  • Canary rollout failures
🎖️

Incident Commander

Leads the response for major incidents. Coordinates teams, communicates to stakeholders, makes decisions.

📝

Scribe

Documents the incident timeline, actions taken, and outcomes. Ensures nothing is forgotten during the chaos.

Response Times

Escalation Timeline

If an alert isn't acknowledged, it automatically escalates to more people at fixed intervals.

0 min — Alert Fires

Prometheus detects the issue and sends it to AlertManager.

0 min — Team Paged

On-call engineer receives a page/notification on their phone.

5 min — First Escalation

If no response, the alert is sent to a backup on-call engineer and the team Slack channel.

15 min — Manager Escalation

If still unacknowledged, the engineering manager and incident commander are looped in.

30 min — Executive Escalation

If no progress, leadership is notified. All hands on deck.
